About

Rural development, agricultural productivity, and policy in China

Xiaoshi Zhou is an associate professor in the Department of Agricultural Economics, College of Economics and Management, China Agricultural University. He is also a master's supervisor. His research focuses on rural development and agricultural productivity.

中文简介

周晓时，中国农业大学经济管理学院农业经济系副教授，硕士生导师。研究方向为农村发展和农业生产率，相关研究发表于 World Development、 Australian Journal of Agricultural and Resource Economics、 《农业技术经济》、《中国农村经济》、《中国农村观察》等期刊。
